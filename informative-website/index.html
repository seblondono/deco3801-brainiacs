Image Contest and Voting System

The Queensland Brain Institute Image Contest

Featuring the most stunning scientific images from QBI researchers voted for by the academic community and general public. This annual competition includes images from the categories: Synaptic Functions, Neurogenesis and Neuronal Survival, Neuronal Development and Connectivity, and Computation and Neuronal Circuits.

Discover the hidden beauty of the brain and its amazing anathomy by selecting your 3 favorite images, and help the important research done at QBI spread across your social networks.
