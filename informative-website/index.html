Image Contest and Voting System

The Queensland Brain Institute Image Contest

Every year the QBI opens a contest featuring the most stunning scientific images from QBI researchers voted for by the academic community and general public. This annual competition includes images from the categories: Synaptic Functions, Neurogenesis and Neuronal Survival, Neuronal Development and Connectivity, and Computation and Neuronal Circuits.

Discover the hidden beauty of the brain and its amazing anathomy by selecting your 3 favorite images, and help the important research done at QBI spread across your social networks.

Image Contest Voting System

Brand new online voting system that allows the general public, the University of Queensland community, and QBI researchers to vote for their 3 favorite images. The system will be open 24/7 and currently allows sing up and login.

In this video you will find a detailed description of the voting system and a walk through the benefits it provides to its three user groups.
